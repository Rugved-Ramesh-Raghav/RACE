About Us

RACE a.k.a. Rugved's Astronomical Center of Exploration is a non-profit, private organization constructed to expand human-cosmological voyages and cement inter-galactic settlements. Originating in Mumbai, India, it has its launch pads, research centres and headquarters all across the globe. Created from a youth's mind (Rugved Raghav), this space-organization aims to promote the interest for the cosmos in children. RACE has partnered several space agencies, such as SPACEX, NASA, ISRO, etc. in each step of their missions and has propelled astronomical technologies by a huge extent. As our motto speaks, "The sky is not our limit" we will never give up on the field of astronomy and we're sure, you'll be interested in joining us too.
